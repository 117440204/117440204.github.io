Travel

Traveling is one of my greatest passions. Over the course of my life, traveling has allowed me to experience loads of different cultures, meet loads of new people, and really experience what our planet has to offer. The 4 cities pictured below are my most memorable adventures.

Glasgow: I visited here to meet some friends I met on Xbox Live at a young age. The city is beautiful, and has some of the best night life I've ever experienced. The people there were so fun, and I got to meet some lifelong friends for the first time.


Havana: The city is full to the brim of beautiful scenery. The contrast of colors made every street feel like a Wes Anderson film. Historically, the city is really interesting too. I got to visit the San Jos√© Market, and met loads of friendly locals.


Kyoto: I have always wanted to visit Japan since I was a child. The Arashiyama Bamboo Grove is to date, the most magical place I've ever been to. The whole experience, from the food, to the culture, to the fashion was really incredible. I really hope to return.


Rio De Janeiro: This journey in particular was very eye-opening. The city is rampant with crime, but it had a really strong sense of community. I really felt at home there, despite the levels of poverty and social issues. It was an experience I will remember for a long time.
